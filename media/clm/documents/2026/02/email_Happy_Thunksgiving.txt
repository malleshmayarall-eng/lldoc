Happy Thunksgiving

The holiday season is fast approaching and it is a good time for us to reflect on things we are thankful for. Recently we announced major milestones ( https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/thunkable-achieves-new-milestones-and-announces-new-product-innovations-301418609.html ) for Thunkable. There are now over 2 million users from 184 different countries using our platform, and turning their ideas into apps. In fact, our users have created over a 6.5+million apps that have been published to PlayStore, AppStore, or downloaded directly onto devices. We are so appreciative of you and your support of Thunkable.

To accompany these milestones, we have some exciting product news to share. These new capabilities are now available on Thunkable:

* *Figma Import* : You can now import a UI designed in Figma directly to Thunkable, to make the design process even faster.
* *WebApps* : Make your app available everywhere by publishing to the web. Put your app on a unique URL or embed it within an iframe.
* *Tablet View* : Create tablet-specific applications to create the perfect user experience based on your device of choice.
* *In-App Payments* : Create a premium experience, charge users for gated features, collect donations, and more with in-app purchasing. Turn your idea into a revenue-generating app.
* *Webflow Integration* : For those of you that want to extend your Webflow project to mobile, you can now use Webflow's back end as a datasource within Thunkbable to keep information consistent and clean, across all devices.

During the season of appreciation – we'd like to say "thank you" for your continued support of Thunkable. Make sure to log in to the Thunkable platform throughout the week. We will be running our Black Friday and Cyber Monday promotions! Take advantage of this once-a-year deal starting on November 24th!
