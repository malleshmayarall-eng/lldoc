Your step-by-step guide to getting ready for pre-order.

It’s easier than ever to get ready for iPhone pre-order. Just pick the model you want and decide how you’d like to pay. This year, you can choose to pay monthly or pay in full. Get the prep work done today so you can speed through checkout on 9.17.

Here’s how it works:

1.

Open the Apple Store app, or visit apple.com.

Don’t have the Apple Store app? Download it now from the App Store.
https://c.apple.com/r?v=2&la=en&lc=usa&a=p3vu%2F%2FkPHE0RCnpWXqV%2Fdj0HNU6stZNGujb2TikduvV2jVUVSAVbB4ZWE6Up5TsRkFo%2FKwIKKoAvLa1QK3xwqiJapzK1kqfPvzUz%2BZ2TKKf5xMe23snc9G6YIx6sAEqg&ct=ac1X4L7c6C

-------------------

2.

Choose the new iPhone you want.

Time for the fun part. Choose between iPhone 13 and iPhone 13 Pro and then select your finish, capacity, and carrier to continue.

-------------------

iPhone 13

From $699 or $29.12/mo. for 24 mo.(1)

iPhone 13 Pro

From $999 or $41.62/mo. for 24 mo.(1)

-------------------

3.

Select the way you’d like to pay.

You can pay in full, pay monthly, and even add a trade-in to get credit toward your new iPhone.(2)

-------------------

4.

Get the paperwork out of the way.

If you plan to pay monthly, you can apply for special financing ahead of time. By filling out a few forms now, you’ll be able to complete your order right away on 9.17.

-------------------

5.

Come back at pre-order and speed through checkout.

Open the Apple Store app or visit apple.com when pre-order begins at 5:00 a.m. PDT on 9.17 and check out with just a few taps. It’s that easy.
