*Activate Your Camera*

Take photos in a snap with the “photo from Camera” block. Great for image sharing apps, or to let your users upload a photo of themselves to their profiles. Check out this doc to learn how you can activate the camera in your app!

Check it out ( https://docs.thunkable.com/v/drag-and-drop/camera )

*Add AI To Your App*

Add Artificial Intelligence to your app with our “description of image” block. This block is powered by Microsoft’s Computer Vision API and allows you to describe the contents of an image with just a single block.

Check it out ( https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zi17wTeUKXs )
